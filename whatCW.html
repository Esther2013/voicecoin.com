What's Conversational Web?

Voiceweb is next generation Web. People use it with wearable devices, smart speakers. When need service, people will not download any app, will not input website address, they just say the service name, then they can use it. Leverage powerful artificial intelligence and blockchain technologies, people will use their voice to sign on the serviec, when they switch the service, user will not sign on again. People only has a unique identity to use all the services, they don't have to register for every services. When use the services, people will mainly use voice. The graphic user interface will all show up when ncessary. People don't need to bind traditional payment tools, people can pay globally, and not worry about trust problems.
