Why do we advocate conversational ?

Web is being used less and less on mobile internet. People use more Apps than web browsers
Now, people are going to use more chatbots than Apps. Most chatbots exist in social media Apps

In the following era of Internet, IoE, people will use more voice to call services. People can't use web in front of a none-interface smart things.
So, the market share of web is going to be shinked to a very small portion.

The entrance of internet will continue being controlled by centralized internet giants

As a result, the following tragidy will continue to happen
- Yahoo accounts
- Wechat security
- ...

How do we restruct web?

Thesically, conversational web is a upgrade of web with blockchain, artificial intellgience and social media factors
